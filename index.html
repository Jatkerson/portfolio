What Is Kerson Games

We are an indie HTML5 game development group that designs free to play online games. All Kerson Games are designed with an emphasis on creating gameplay that requires a high level of diplomatic strategy amoungst players. Our free to play games borrow the best features of the Real Time Strategy (RTS), Turn Based Strategy (TBS) and Online Role Playing Games (ORPG) genres to create online games with depth. Kerson Games are developed in web languages that work in all modern browsers and do not require an installation process or additional plugins to play.
